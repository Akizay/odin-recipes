Nasi Goreng

Description

Originating from Indonesia, this humble dish is a common staple and never disappoints. The umami flavors of the soy sauce, the spicy kick from the Thai chili's, and the freshness from the vegetables all work in harmony.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Cooling oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Protein source
  • Thai chilis
  • Green onions
  • Fresh vegetables
  • Any spices you like

Steps

  1. Add cooking oil and garlic to a pan
  2. Heat the pan on medium heat
  3. Once sizzling, add the cooked rice and soy sauce (and protein source if needed) and stir
  4. After a minute, add Thai chilis and green onions and stir
  5. Once it is cooked, plate the fried rice
  6. Add any fresh vegetables
  7. Enjoy!
